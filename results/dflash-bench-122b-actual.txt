Recommendation: k=5 on this hardware. k=15 is the upstream-recommended default
(designed for B200 with fa3 + cheap verifier) but on single-Spark INT4-Hybrid the
verifier cost dominates by k=15 — DFlash drafts 15 tokens, vLLM accepts ~3-4,
the rest are wasted forward-pass work.

Master branch (single-Spark, same hybrid+INT8 LM Head, MTP-2) records 51 tok/s.
DFlash on this hardware does NOT beat master at any k tested.

Headline (k=5): 43.4 tok/s mean, 51.6 tok/s peak (LongCode). Master MTP-2 is 51 tok/s
mean, ~52 tok/s peak. DFlash and MTP-2 converge on long repetitive output but
DFlash trails on short answers by ~10 tok/s due to the larger fixed draft block.
